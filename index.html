All Subjects Syllabus

This section provides a consolidated view of the syllabus for all subjects covered in the CGL exam.

  • General Intelligence and Reasoning: Includes topics like puzzles, analogy, coding-decoding, and classification.
  • General Awareness: Focus on current events, history, geography, and general science.
  • Quantitative Aptitude: Covers arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and advanced mathematics.
  • English Comprehension: Topics include grammar, vocabulary, and reading comprehension.
  • Statistics: Includes probability, data interpretation, and sampling techniques.
  • General Studies (Finance and Economics): Basics of accounting, macroeconomics, and fiscal policy.

Strategy for CGL 2025

Here are some tips and strategies to help you prepare effectively for CGL 2025:

  • Understand the Syllabus: Familiarize yourself with the complete syllabus and exam pattern to avoid any surprises.
  • Time Management: Create a study schedule that allocates time to each subject based on its difficulty level and your comfort with it.
  • Focus on Weak Areas: Identify your weak subjects and allocate more time to them while revising stronger subjects periodically.
  • Practice Mock Tests: Take regular mock tests to simulate exam conditions and improve speed and accuracy.
  • Current Affairs: Stay updated on current affairs as General Awareness is a key part of the exam.
  • Revision: Regularly revise important topics, especially in Quantitative Aptitude and Reasoning, to retain concepts.
